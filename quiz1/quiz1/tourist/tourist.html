LOCAL TOURISM

BAWEAN

One of the most attractive places to be used as tourist attractions on the Pulau Bawean Gresik or Bawean Island. This island offers the beauty of clean white sand and has a clean sea compared to other seas on the north coast of Java. On Bawean Island, you can see a rare species of deer, namely the Bawean Deer. The location of this island is quite far, which is about 120 Km from the center of Gresik City. From the port, you can use a speedboat to go faster to Bawean Island. FYI, this island is one of the tourist attractions in Gresik which is famous for having various amazing attractions.

BUKIT JAMUR

This Bukit Jamur Gresik tourist location is located in Bunga District, Gresik Regency, East Java. If you go there, you will find mounds of earth that resemble a giant mushroom, just like the house of the dwarves. What makes it unique is that the top of the mushroom is a large rock supported by a pile of soil shaped like a mushroom stalk.

PANTAI DELEGAN

One of the other beaches in Gresik is Pantai Delegan or Delegan Beach. This beach is located in Gelengan Village, Panceng District, Gresik Regency. This beach has beautiful white sand and calm waves, so it is not dangerous to be used as a place to swim. This beach is famous as a tourist destination for families, especially children. On this beach, children can play on the soft white sand of the beach, play sand castles and dip their feet into the sand. In addition, children can also play in the water and swim on the beach. That's why this beach is often used as a family tour while on vacation. This beach is one of the tourist destinations of Gresik Lamongan.

SETIGI

Wisata Setigi or Setigi Gresik Tourism offers a unique concept similar to Breksi Cliff Tourism in Jogja, which is to present natural beauty with tourist buildings plus instagramable photo spots. This new Gresik tourist spot is located in the Sekapuk Village area, Ujungpangkah District, Gresik. That is, it is about 27 kilometers from the Manyar toll exit, and can be reached by four-wheeled vehicles for 40 minutes. Sekapuk Setigi Kapur Hill is also very suitable as a place to unwind from the hustle and bustle of the city. Because there tourists will be spoiled by the charm of beauty that is so charming.
