LOCAL CUISINE

Pudak

Pudak is a snack from Gresik made from rice flour, brown sugar/sugar and coconut milk wrapped in betel leaf midrib. As the name implies, the taste of this dish is sweet and has a soft texture. One of the sweet snacks from Gresik.

Otak Otak Bandeng

One of the other processed forms is Otak Otak Bandeng. Otak Otak Bandeng is a processed milkfish obtained by mixing milkfish meat with spices so that a new product is obtained without losing the distinctive taste of milkfish. Otak Otak Bandeng from Gresik has a peculiarity in the form of the spices used tend to be strong and taste spicy, but still do not eliminate the taste of the milkfish.

Nasi Krawu

Nasi Krawu is a typical Gresik rice preparation consisting of white fluffier rice with a side dish in the form of shredded meat and sometimes offal seasoned with special spices that will produce a sweet savory taste. In addition to shredded meat, there is also serundeng made from grated coconut and Sambal Petis Udang. Nasi krawu often served uses banana leaves and sometimes other side dishes are added such as tempeh, tofu, bacem and various types of satay.

Bonggolan

Bonggolan is an oval -shaped snack made from the basic ingredients of fish meat mixed with starch flour added with salt and garlic which is then steamed until cooked. This snack has a chewy texture. Bonggolan are usually served fried. Eaten warm and with a companion sauce.
