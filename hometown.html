GRESIK

Gresik is my hometown, a city that tends to be famous for its cement, even though Gresik is also the city of Wali Songo, a city that produces milkfish and many more. Gresik is a city close to the sea, so from an economic point of view, we are no less than Surabaya. Because there are also many production factories and the minimum wage is high.
